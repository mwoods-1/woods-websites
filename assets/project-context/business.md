# Woods Websites — The Business

## What It Is

Woods Websites is a web design and development business run by two brothers, Sean and Mark Woods. Founded in 2024.

## What We Do

We design, build, and maintain websites for small-to-medium businesses. Our services include:

- Custom website design and development
- Website redesigns and modernization
- Third-party platform integrations (booking systems, payment processors, etc.)
- Performance optimization and SEO
- Ongoing website maintenance and support

## Who We Work With

Businesses across industries — hospitality, tourism, sports organizations, property services, and others. Our clients so far:

- **Aviation Expeditions** — Alaskan flightseeing tour company (redesign)
- **Sven's Basecamp Hostel** — Hostel in Fairbanks, Alaska (redesign + Cloudbeds booking integration)
- **Ovens Soccer** — Sports organization (new build)
- **Kingdom Property Care** — Property maintenance company (new build)
