# Woods Websites — Website Purpose

## Why This Website Exists

This is our portfolio and lead-generation site. Its job is to:

1. **Show off our work** — Demonstrate to potential clients that we build high-quality, professional websites
2. **Generate leads** — Give visitors an easy way to reach out about their project
3. **Establish credibility** — Look polished enough that a business owner trusts us to build *their* site
4. **Be a showcase of our skill** — The site itself should be impressive. If we're selling web design, our own site needs to be undeniably good

## Who Visits It

- Business owners considering a new website or a redesign
- People we've sent the link to directly after a conversation
- Anyone Googling for web design services

## What It Needs to Accomplish

- Make a strong first impression — visitors should immediately think "these guys know what they're doing"
- Show real client work with enough detail that prospects can picture what we'd do for them
- Make it dead simple to get in touch (contact form, email)
- Work flawlessly on mobile — if we're selling responsive design, ours better be perfect
- Load fast — if we're selling performance, ours needs to be fast

## Pages

- **Homepage** — First impression, signature moment, preview of work and services
- **Portfolio** — All client projects with screenshots and descriptions
- **Services** — What we offer and how we work
- **About** — Who we are, our values, our skills
- **Contact** — Form to start a conversation
- **Privacy Policy** — Legal requirement
- **Terms of Service** — Legal requirement

## The Scroll-Stop Animation

We built a signature animation called "The Workshop" — a scroll-driven scene where a monitor with floating tech cards (Code, Design, Speed, Next.js, Tailwind, etc.) emerges as the user scrolls. This is the one element we want to keep. The standalone code is saved at `assets/scroll-stop-animation/WorkshopSection.tsx`.
